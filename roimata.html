Roimata is an augmented reality fairytale about a girl whose tears grow plants and bring life to the land. After a deposed king takes over the village, Roimata must return to rid the land of the king and save her village. Animation done in Adobe Animate, illustrations done in Adobe Photoshop, and augmented reality programming done using Vuforia in Unity.
