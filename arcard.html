Combining my two favorite things, baseball and augmented reality technology. The baseball card, when put up to a phone, will embed a highlight reel of the player over the card. This reel can always be updated within the app so that users are getting the latest content. Future features will include an updated and real-time statistics sheet on the back. This will be updated through an online baseball stats database. AR done using Vuforia in Unity.
