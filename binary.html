Binary is an interactive comic built in Adobe Animate. It tells the story of an android that is being reviewed for commission into the world. Things are strange about this model though. The comic is in black and white to emphasize the black & white, binary decisions that a robot can make and why the real world doesn't allow for that simplistic outlook.
